Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025
Important: This Privacy Policy explains how Maharashtra Fort Guide ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location Information

Maharashtra Fort Guide requires location access to provide core functionalities:

  • GPS Location: To show your current position on maps and provide directions to heritage sites
  • Distance Calculations: To calculate distances to forts, caves, and waterfalls
  • Emergency Services: To share your location during SOS emergencies
  • Weather Data: To provide location-specific weather information

1.2 Device Information

We may collect device-related information including:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version and usage statistics
  • Language preferences
  • Network connectivity status

1.3 Emergency Contact Information

For the SOS feature, we store:

  • Emergency contact names and phone numbers (stored locally on your device)
  • Emergency preferences and settings

1.4 App Usage Data

We collect anonymized usage statistics to improve the app:

  • Features used and frequency
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Performance metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core App Functionality

  • Navigation: Provide directions and location-based services
  • Safety: Enable emergency SOS features and location sharing
  • Personalization: Customize content based on your location and preferences
  • Weather Services: Provide accurate weather information for trekking

2.2 App Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Fix bugs and improve app stability
  • Develop new features based on user needs

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Emergency contacts and preferences
  • App settings and customizations
  • Downloaded map data and heritage site information
  • Search history and favorites

3.2 Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

  • Encrypted local storage for sensitive information
  • Secure transmission protocols for network communications
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Minimal data collection principle

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements:

  • AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization
  • You can opt-out of personalized ads in your device settings
  • Ads are displayed in non-intrusive locations within the app

4.2 Weather Services

We integrate with weather APIs to provide accurate forecasts:

  • Location data is shared with weather services to get relevant forecasts
  • No personal information beyond location is shared

4.3 Maps and Location Services

We use OpenStreetMap and Google Play Services:

  • Location data for map rendering and navigation
  • These services have their own privacy policies

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Emergency Situations: Location data shared during SOS activation with your designated emergency contacts
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect safety
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help operate our app (weather services, ads)

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Location Permissions

  • You can revoke location permissions in your device settings
  • Note: Core app functionality requires location access
  • You can choose precision level (precise vs. approximate location)

6.2 Data Management

  • Access: View your stored data within the app settings
  • Delete: Clear app data or uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Modify: Update emergency contacts and preferences anytime

6.3 Advertising Choices

  • Opt-out of personalized ads in your device's advertising settings
  • Use "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS or "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android

7. Children's Privacy

Maharashtra Fort Guide is suitable for all ages but does not specifically target children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. International Users

If you are using our app from outside India, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in India. By using our app, you consent to this transfer and processing.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Updated privacy policy with revision date
  • Email notifications (if contact information is provided)

10. Offline Functionality

Maharashtra Fort Guide works offline for core features:

  • Downloaded heritage site information remains accessible
  • Emergency contacts and SOS features work without internet
  • Map data can be cached for offline use
  • No data is transmitted when offline
